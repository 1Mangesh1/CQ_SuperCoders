About Our Todo App

Our todo app is a simple and easy-to-use tool for managing your tasks and staying organized. With our app, you can:

  • Add new tasks
  • Mark tasks as complete
  • Delete tasks
  • Filter tasks by status (completed or not completed)

Our app is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, so you can start using it right away without any training or tutorials. Try it out today and see how it can help you stay on top of your tasks!
